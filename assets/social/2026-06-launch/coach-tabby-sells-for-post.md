## TikTok / Reel (text-over or talking-head; no thrift footage needed)
> That Coach Tabby listed for $365? It actually sells for about $198.
>
> The listing price is a hope. The sold price is the fact. On eBay's completed sales, the Tabby changes hands around $200, basically the same whether it's the 20, the 26, or the shoulder bag. So when a reseller's asking $365, there's room to offer, and plenty of the bag at the lower number.
>
> One Coach that does hold more: the Rogue. Glovetanned leather, a structured frame, made in smaller numbers, so it sits around $500 and up. Two to three times a Tabby, from the same brand.
>
> Why the split? Volume and trend pull resale down. Leather and scarcity hold it up.

**Outro:** Pay the sold price, not the asking price. Full breakdown, every size, at the link. Follow for more where handbags meet data, I put it all on LuxuryCatalog.com so you can spend smart.
