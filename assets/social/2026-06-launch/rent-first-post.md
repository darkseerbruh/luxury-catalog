## TikTok / Reel (her on camera; ties to her real "renting taught me what I need" story)
> Before you drop four figures on a bag, rent it first. I mean it.
>
> Renting and buying answer different questions. Buying wins when you'll actually carry it, a lot, because the cost per wear drops the more you use it. Renting wins when you chase trends, need an occasion bag, or just want to try one on your arm before you commit.
>
> Here's the test: Will this bag live in your daily rotation, or get carried twice? Daily driver, buy it, and buy it smart on resale. A few times a year, rent it.
>
> I learned my own deal-breakers by renting first. Turns out I need a top handle, a structured base, and a crossbody strap, and I only knew that because I tried before I bought. It saved me from some expensive mistakes.
