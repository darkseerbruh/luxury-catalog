## TikTok / Reel (open with hook A or C, then:)
> Birkin or Kelly. People agonize over this for years. So first, the part that should end half the arguments: on resale they cost almost the same. A 25 of either runs around $25,000. This was never about money.
>
> It's about how you actually carry it, and here's where I get opinionated. The Birkin is an open tote, two handles, throw it on, reach right in. Gorgeous. But it has no crossbody strap, on a bag that costs this much, which is wild to me. People literally pay to add aftermarket straps.
>
> The Kelly does have a strap, and it's the dressier, more structured one. But leave it unlocked for easy access and, in my experience, it flops forward and your stuff slides out.
>
> So pick your flaw. Hands-free and structured, and you'll live with the flop? Kelly. Just want to reach in, and you'll carry it on your arm forever? Birkin.

**Outro:** Either way you're not overpaying for one over the other, it's a wash. Full size-by-size at the link. Follow for real bag data with the gatekeeping cut out, LuxuryCatalog.com.
